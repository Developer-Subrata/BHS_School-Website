Vocational Education

Offered under the West Bengal State Council of Technical & Vocational Education and Skill Development (WBSCTVESD)

Computer Applications & Science

Basic programming, digital tools, and IT operations with hands-on labs.

  • Programming fundamentals (Python / C / JavaScript)
  • Databases, web development, and version control (Git)
  • Operating systems, networking, and cybersecurity basics

Electrical Engineering (Diploma)

Core theory and lab practice for power, electronics and industrial control.

  • DC/AC circuits, machines, transformers & power systems
  • Analog/digital electronics, PCB prototyping & testing
  • PLC, sensors, industrial safety & maintenance

Civil Engineering (Diploma)

Site-ready skills for structures, surveying, materials and project control.

  • Construction materials & testing (concrete, steel, soils)
  • AutoCAD drafting, leveling & total-station surveying
  • Estimation, BOQ, contracts & site safety management

Automobile Engineering (Diploma)

Design, manufacturing and maintenance of vehicles with strong workshop practice.

  • Engine systems, transmission, braking & EV fundamentals
  • AutoCAD/CAE basics, metrology & quality control
  • Workshop: diagnosis, service & overhauling

Each course includes both academic and hands-on training, with assessments conducted by WBSCTVESD. Successful candidates receive a recognized Higher Secondary (Vocational) certificate.
