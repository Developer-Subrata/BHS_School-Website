Arts Stream

Affiliated to the West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education (WBCHSE)

Higher Secondary Arts Education

The Arts stream at Bhagabangola Government High School is designed to nurture creativity, critical thinking, and cultural awareness. It follows the curriculum prescribed by the West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education (WBCHSE) for Classes XI and XII.

  • Core Subjects: Bengali, English
  • Electives: History, Geography, Political Science, Philosophy, Education, Sanskrit
  • New Subjects (2025–26): Basic Mathematics for Social Sciences (BMSS)

Students gain in-depth understanding of society, culture, politics, and human values. The curriculum also supports preparation for competitive exams and higher studies in humanities, law, education, civil services, and more.

Assessment includes internal evaluation, project work, and annual WBCHSE board exams. Regular classes, debates, essay writing, and field activities enhance the overall learning experience.
